The Legacy

25 Years of Heritage
Meets Modern Elegance

IVANTA is the exclusive luxury sub-brand of Indus Footwear, a renowned name with over 25 years of established heritage in exceptional shoemaking.

While Indus Footwear has built a decades-long legacy of trust and quality, IVANTA was launched just a year ago with a highly specialized focus: to master the art of premium handcrafted loafers and architectural boots. We source only the finest full-grain leathers and premium suedes to ensure each pair is flawless.

We believe that true luxury lies in the details—from the precision of our stitching to the structural balance of our heels. IVANTA is more than footwear; it is the modern pinnacle of the 25-year Indus legacy.
